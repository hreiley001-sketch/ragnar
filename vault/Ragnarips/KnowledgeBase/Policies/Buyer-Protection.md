# Buyer Protection

**Rule:** Buyers who pay through RAGNAR checkout are covered when an item is significantly not as described, never ships, or is confirmed counterfeit/fraud after evidence review.

**Applies to:** Orders with status paid, shipped, delivered, or disputed. Live-ride wins follow the same fraud path but completed authentic wins are generally final except for proven fraud.

**Window:** Open a dispute from Account → Orders as soon as you have evidence. Counsel escalates fraud / counterfeit keywords immediately.

**Agent action:**
1. Confirm order identity and payment.
2. Collect photos, tracking, and seller communication.
3. Auto-escalate to fraud queue if keywords include fraud, stolen, counterfeit, fake slab.
4. Otherwise open/keep dispute and notify seller; target 72h seller response.
5. Staff resolve refund or deny; never improvise outside this policy.
