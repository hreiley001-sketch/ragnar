# Flow Principles

The vault’s constitution. Short. Stable. Revisited, not rewritten weekly.

## 1. Flow over form

If a structure slows capture, simplify it. Folders are scaffolding, not the building.

## 2. Cohesive system thinking

Every note belongs to a larger ecosystem: [[Daily]] ↔ [[Evergreen/Index|Evergreen]] ↔ [[Projects/Index|Projects]] ↔ [[Maps/Atlas|Maps]].

## 3. Minimal but powerful

Six homes only: Inbox, Daily, Evergreen, Projects, Maps, System. Links and metadata do the heavy lifting.

## 4. Creative intelligence

Prefer clarity, patterns, and conceptual links over exhaustive filing.

## 5. Obsessive interconnection

No orphans. New notes get at least one meaningful link. Maps absorb clusters.

## 6. Workflow smoothness

Templates reduce friction. Reviews keep the graph alive. See [[System/Capture Flow]] and [[System/Weekly Review]].

## 7. Unbranded energy

Clean. Intentional. Cohesive. No aesthetic noise — just unified mind.

## 8. Teach as you build

[[System/Skill Path]] grows mastery in steps, not dumps.
