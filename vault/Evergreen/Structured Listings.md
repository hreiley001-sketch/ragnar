# Structured Listings

A listing is a structured claim about a card — not a free-text blob.

## Core fields

Category, set, card number, player/character, year, and either raw condition **or** grader + grade.

## Why structure wins

Search, comps, and trust all depend on shared identity. Structure is the shared language between seller, buyer, and market data.
