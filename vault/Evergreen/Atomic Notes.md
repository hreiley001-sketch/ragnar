# Atomic Notes

One idea per note. Title is a claim or concept, not a topic dump.

## Why

Atomic notes make linking honest. If a note tries to hold five ideas, connections become vague.

## Practice

- Write the idea in your own words
- Link to at least two neighbors when you create it
- Split when a section wants its own title
