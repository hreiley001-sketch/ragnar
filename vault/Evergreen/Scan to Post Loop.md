# Scan to Post Loop

Photo → identity → prefill → comps → list. One continuous glide.

## Intent

Remove the grind between “I have this card” and “it’s listed correctly.”

## Flow

1. Upload photo
2. Recognition proposes identity
3. Structured fields prefill
4. Sold history / comps suggest price
5. Seller confirms and posts

## Principle

Automation proposes; the human affirms. Trust stays human-owned.
