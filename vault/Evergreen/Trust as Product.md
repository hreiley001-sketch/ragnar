# Trust as Product

In a collector marketplace, trust is not a feature bolted on — it is the product surface.

## Shape

- Structured, grading-aware listings reduce ambiguity
- Clear fees reduce suspicion
- Buyer protection + Counsel turn conflict into process

## Tension

Speed of posting vs. quality of structure. Scan-to-post should accelerate honesty, not skip it.
