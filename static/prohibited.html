Prohibited Items & Conduct

Last updated: July 23, 2026 · Operated by RAGNAR (ragnarips.com)

Banned itemsBanned conductEnforcement
Violations can lead to listing removal, account suspension, or bans. See also Terms.

1. Banned items

  • Counterfeit, replica, or fake cards presented as authentic.
  • Altered grades or tampered slabs presented as genuine graded product.
  • Stolen goods.
  • Non-collectible goods unrelated to the trading-card marketplace (unless we explicitly expand categories).
  • Items that violate law or card-company / IP rules applicable to your jurisdiction.

2. Banned conduct

  • Shill bidding, fake tracking, triangulation fraud, or payment fraud.
  • Harassment, threats, or abuse of buyers, sellers, or staff.
  • Attempting to move paid transactions off-platform to avoid fees or protections.
  • Repeatedly filing abusive refund or chargeback claims.

3. Enforcement

We may remove content, cancel orders, withhold payouts, suspend accounts, and report criminal activity. Fraud rings and legal threats are escalated immediately to human review.

Report concerns via Support or henry@ragnarips.com.
