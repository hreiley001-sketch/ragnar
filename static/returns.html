Returns support Buyer Protection outcomes. Money movement is covered in Refunds.

1. When a return is required

For eligible not-as-described or damaged-in-transit cases above our keep-the-item threshold, we may require the buyer to return the item before or as part of the refund.

2. Prepaid labels

  • For eligible cases, RAGNAR may generate a prepaid return label.
  • Ship within the timeframe given in your case instructions.
  • Refunds typically trigger when the return is scanned or delivered (case-dependent).

3. Keep-the-item refunds

For low-value items under $25, we may refund without requiring a return (you keep the item). Support will say so in the case outcome.

4. Buyer’s remorse

Remorse returns are not guaranteed. If a seller opts in, a restocking fee (typically around 10%) may apply. Items must be returned in the same condition.

5. How to start

Open a case in Support with your order number and photos. Do not discard packaging until the case is resolved when damage is claimed.
