Shipping Policy

Clear shipping keeps Buyer Protection working. Related: Buyer Protection · Returns · Refunds.

1. For buyers

  • Shipping cost (if any) is shown on the listing and at checkout.
  • After the seller marks an order shipped, tracking appears under Account → Orders.
  • Carriers can take 24–48 hours to show the first scan.
  • If tracking has not updated for 10+ days after the ship date, open a not-received case in Support.

2. For sellers — timelines

  • Ship within 3 business days of payment (or the window stated on your listing, if shorter).
  • Upload a valid carrier tracking number when you mark shipped.
  • Use sturdy packaging suitable for cards and slabs (sleeves, toploaders/team bags, rigid mailers or boxes). Avoid bending.
  • High-value items should use tracked and insured service appropriate to the item value.
  • Repeated late shipping or fake tracking can lead to account limits or removal.

3. Methods, rates & insurance

Sellers set shipping prices or offer free shipping. When label tools are enabled on the Platform, rates come from carrier integrations (e.g. Shippo). Insurance and signature confirmation are recommended for higher-value shipments; sellers remain responsible for packing and carrier selection.

4. International

International shipping is allowed only when the seller explicitly offers it. Buyers are responsible for customs duties and import taxes. Delivery times vary by destination and customs.

5. Delays, loss & damage

  • Carrier delays alone are not automatic refunds — we investigate using tracking and seller evidence.
  • Lost or damaged in transit: open a case promptly with photos (for damage) via Support.
  • Refund and return outcomes follow Refunds and Returns.
