AI Tools

AI built into every step

RAGNAR uses AI to help you find cards, list them fast, price them fairly, design your store, get support, and ship orders — without the busywork. Here's every tool and how to use it.

Open the marketplace →

💬 Ask RAGNAR

Where: the “Ask RAGNAR” button, bottom-right of every page

Your shopping concierge. Describe what you want in plain language — a player, a set, a budget, a vibe — and it turns that into a filtered marketplace search. It can also restyle your own view (colors, dark mode) just for you.

  1. Click Ask RAGNAR (bottom-right).
  2. Type what you're after, e.g. a player + budget.
  3. It takes you to matching results in the marketplace.

“Cheap PSA 10 rookies under $50” · “A gift for a Lakers fan” · “Make my view dark”

📷 Scan-to-List

Where: the “⚡ Sell” button → snap or upload a photo

Turn a photo of a card into a ready-to-post listing. The scanner reads the card and auto-fills set, player/character, year, grade, and category — so you're not typing every field.

  1. Click ⚡ Sell in the header.
  2. Add a photo of the card (or its slab).
  3. Review the auto-filled details and post.

Works best with a clear, straight-on photo and readable card text.

💲 AI Pricing & Comps

Where: inside the listing flow, after the card is identified

Price with confidence. RAGNAR suggests a price from recent sold comps for the same card, set, and grade — and shows the sold history behind it so you can adjust.

  1. Identify the card (via Scan-to-List or manually).
  2. See the suggested price and recent sold comps.
  3. Accept it or set your own.

“Suggested: $412 — based on 6 recent PSA 9 sales.”

Browse priced cards

🎨 Store Designer

Where: My Store → Store Designer chat

Design your storefront by describing a vibe. The designer suggests an accent color, tagline, and bio to match — you preview it and apply in one click. No design skills needed.

  1. Go to My Store.
  2. Tell the designer your vibe.
  3. Preview the look and apply it.

“Clean and premium for high-end vintage” · “Bright and fun for Pokémon”

Open My Store

🛟 Counsel — AI Support

Where: the Support page

Resolution without the ticket queue. Counsel tracks orders, starts refunds and returns, and answers policy questions — acting when the answer is clear and escalating only the edge cases to a human.

  1. Open Support.
  2. Describe the issue (include an order # if you have one).
  3. Counsel resolves it or routes it to the team.

“Where's order #10423?” · “Start a return for a damaged card.”

Open Support

📦 Dispatch — AI Shipping

Where: the Shipping / Dispatch page

Fulfillment without the busywork. Dispatch quotes rates, recommends card packaging, buys labels, marks orders shipped, tracks packages, and flags lost/stale shipments — the full outbound pipeline.

  1. Open Dispatch.
  2. Ask for orders to ship, a rate quote, or a label (include order #).
  3. Dispatch packs, purchases postage, and notifies the buyer.

“Orders to ship” · “Create a label for order #1042” · “How should I pack a slab?”

Open Dispatch

✨ RAGNAR Studio · staff

Where: the “Studio” button (staff accounts only)

For the RAGNAR team: reshape the whole site — colors, fonts, announcements, and landing copy — in plain English, preview it live, and publish in one tap.

“Announce the Friday live drop” · “Bolder headline about lower fees”

Every AI tool degrades gracefully: if the AI service is briefly unavailable, RAGNAR falls back to a fast rules-based version so the feature still works. AI suggestions are assistive — you're always in control of the final listing, price, and store.
