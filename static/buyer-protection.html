Every paid order on RAGNAR is covered by Buyer Protection. File claims in Support. Details also live in Refunds, Returns, and Shipping.

1. What is covered

  • Not received — paid item never arrives after a reasonable shipping window / stalled tracking.
  • Damaged — item arrives damaged in transit.
  • Significantly not as described — material mismatch vs listing (including serious grade/condition issues).

We resolve most cases with automation; complex, high-value, legal, or fraud cases go to a human review queue.

2. What is not covered

  • Changing your mind after a fair, as-described delivery (unless a seller remorse-return path exists).
  • Minor subjective preferences (e.g. centering taste) when the listing photos and description were accurate.
  • Issues reported outside the refund windows.
  • Transactions completed off-platform.

3. How claims work

  1. Contact Support with your order ID and evidence.
  2. We may pause payouts, request seller evidence, and arrange a return label.
  3. Approved outcomes: full/partial refund and/or return, per policy.

4. Seller role

By selling on RAGNAR you agree that Buyer Protection decisions may reverse or adjust payouts. Accurate listings, fast shipping, and good packing are the best defense.
