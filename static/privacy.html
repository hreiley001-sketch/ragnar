This Privacy Policy explains how RAGNAR (ragnarips.com) collects, uses, and shares personal information when you use the Platform. Related details: Cookie Policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data — name, email, password hash, profile/store details, Google account identifiers if you use Google sign-in.
  • Transaction data — orders, offers, bids, payouts, shipping addresses, and payment metadata processed by Stripe (we do not store full card numbers).
  • Content you provide — listings, photos, messages, support chats, applications.
  • Usage & device data — IP address, browser type, pages viewed, approximate location, and diagnostics.
  • Communications — emails, notifications preferences, and Counsel conversations.

2. How we use information

  • Operate the marketplace (accounts, listings, checkout, shipping, disputes).
  • Provide AI features (scan-to-list, pricing suggestions, search, support) using the content you submit.
  • Prevent fraud, enforce policies, and keep the Platform secure.
  • Send transactional email (verification, receipts, shipping updates) and optional marketing if you opt in.
  • Improve product performance and reliability.

3. Sharing

We share data with:

  • Other users as needed for a sale (seller sees shipping info; public storefront fields you choose to show).
  • Service providers such as Stripe (payments), email delivery, cloud hosting, image/CDN tools, analytics (when configured), and shipping label providers.
  • Legal / safety when required by law or to protect rights, users, or the Platform.

We do not sell personal information.

4. Cookies & similar tech

We use cookies and local storage for sessions, preferences, and (when enabled) analytics. See Cookie Policy.

5. Retention

We keep account and transaction records as long as needed to operate the Platform, meet legal/tax obligations, and resolve disputes. You may request deletion subject to those obligations.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data, or to object to certain processing. Contact henry@ragnarips.com. You can also manage marketing opt-in in account settings and close your account by contacting support.

7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards (encrypted transport, hashed passwords, access controls). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Children

The Platform is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 where required). We do not knowingly collect their personal information.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: henry@ragnarips.com.
