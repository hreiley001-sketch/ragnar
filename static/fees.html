Fees

Last updated: July 23, 2026 · Operated by RAGNAR (ragnarips.com)

Seller feesProcessingShippingRefunds & chargebacksWhy it matters
Simple fees are part of the product. Quotes in cart and listing flows use these rates. Founding status is assigned to the first 250 approved sellers.

1. Seller platform fees

  • Standard: flat 5% of the item sale price.
  • Founding 250: flat 4% forever for the first 250 sellers — no expiry, no sales cap.
  • No insertion fees for listing.

2. Payment processing

Card processing is pass-through (typically 2.9% + $0.30 via Stripe). Shown separately from the platform fee in fee quotes.

3. Shipping

Set by the seller (flat, free, or calculated). Not a RAGNAR commission.

4. Refunds & chargebacks

When an order is refunded, platform and processing fee treatment follows the payment provider and case outcome. Chargebacks may include network fees passed through to the responsible party when applicable.

5. Why it matters

Many general marketplaces charge higher final-value fees on collectibles. RAGNAR keeps a flat, transparent take rate so sellers can price with confidence. See Become a seller.
