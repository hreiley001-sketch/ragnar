This Cookie Policy explains how RAGNAR uses cookies and similar technologies. Read with our Privacy Policy.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We also use local storage and similar technologies for sessions and preferences.

2. Types we use

  • Essential — sign-in session cookies, security, load balancing. Required for the site to work.
  • Preferences — theme, dismissed onboarding tips, local UI choices.
  • Analytics — optional product analytics (e.g. PostHog) when configured, to understand feature usage.

3. Managing cookies

You can clear or block cookies in your browser. Blocking essential cookies may break sign-in and checkout. Marketing emails are controlled by your opt-in preference, not cookies alone.
