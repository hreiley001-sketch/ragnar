Refunds work together with Buyer Protection and Returns. Most cases start in Support.

1. When refunds apply

  • Item never arrived (not received).
  • Item arrived damaged in transit.
  • Item is significantly not as described (including material grade/condition misrepresentation).
  • Seller cancellation after payment (buyer is made whole).

2. Time windows

  • Within 30 days of delivery for delivered orders.
  • Within 45 days of payment if the item was never delivered.
  • Open cases sooner when tracking stalls (see Shipping).

3. What is refunded

  • Full refunds typically cover item price + original shipping paid by the buyer.
  • Partial refunds may apply for minor condition issues when both parties agree or Support decides.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method when possible (Stripe). Timing depends on the bank/card network.

4. Exclusions

  • Buyer’s remorse after delivery (unless the seller opts into a remorse return with possible restocking — see Returns).
  • Completed live-ride wins, except fraud or material misrepresentation.
  • Digital goods / non-physical entitlements, except fraud.
  • Abuse: buyers are limited to 3 AI-approved refunds in a rolling 30-day window; further cases go to human review.

5. How to request

  1. Go to Support or Account → Orders.
  2. Share the order number, issue type, and evidence (photos, tracking).
  3. Counsel may resolve automatically or escalate to a human queue for high-value, legal, or fraud cases.

6. Seller impact

Approved refunds may reverse seller payouts or create a balance adjustment via Stripe. Repeated defects, non-shipment, or policy violations can affect seller standing.
